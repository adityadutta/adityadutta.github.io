Bio

About Me

I am Aditya Dutta. I was born in New Delhi, India. I have been programming since the age 12.I was excellent in solving puzzles with my general instincts since I was 3 years old. With time passing by, I decided to study Science in high school to finally pursue a career in the gaming industry. With an analytical mindset studying game programming will enable me to further enhance my ability and urge to innovate and create something new.

Why did I chose Game Development?

As a young boy, I was always fascinated by games and whenever got a chance, I used to enjoy playing various games available in video parlours in my neighbourhood market or during a local tour with my parents. Thereafter, I started spending hours exploring new games in my computer. The activity never lost its zest and as I grew up, I knew that I wished to become a game developer and started working towards my dream of developing games for the best names in the world. Gradually, with the growing passion, I have transformed my personal computer to a “Gaming PC” myself. I was confident about my choice and knew that it is the best option as per my interest and ability.

The game which made me choose a career in game programming is Assassins Creed Revelations.

I got the game on my birthday in April 2012. While playing it, I just fell in love with the gameplay and storyline. The protagonists, Ezio and Altair, made me feel like I was a part of the creed. The game was rated the “Best Assassins Creed Game” by IGN.

The gameplay was so smooth that I didn’t notice sometimes that I was playing a game. The game mechanics were so advanced. The graphics were just mesmerizing and soundtrack was great. I then realized that I would also make a game like Assassins Creed and hence, my dream is to join Ubisoft and work on an Assassins Creed series project as a Gameplay Programmer.
